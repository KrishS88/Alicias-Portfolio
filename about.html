About Me

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I was born and raised in Stockholm, Sweden, residing here since 2006. I am Norwegian and Croatian, and my trips to Croatia is where my spark for pursuing digital art arised from my grandfather, Vladimir Beck, a professional painter.

My passion for art begun at a very young age, and ever since I've used the pen to convey my feelings. My pieces are extremely diverse, some are works made for friends as a sign of my gratitude, some works are used for video games and animations, and some pieces were created from raw emotion. I primarily work on my tablet with ProCreate since the direct "pen on paper" contact feels generally better. Two dimensional art is where I deliver my best work, however I am very eager to dive into three dimensional works in the future.

I plan to expand on my virtual and digital artistic abilities in hopes to explore anything involving art in growing fields. I am most interested in Concept art design for videogames or other media, as my brainstorming for finished products would be at its maximum potential.
